Olenka is a graphic designer based in NY and RVA. This is her landing page


Olenka's work explores the concept of modularity and both its conceptual and material application in visual identities.
She is currently a Letter Press Intern at Studio Two Three, and recently designed a poster for O+M Lecture Series at VCU.

She is available for freelance graphic design work.

She really likes sfogliatelle and bodybuilding. She also has a cat named Square.

Modularity refers to a system's ability to be broken down into distinct, independent parts. Like a tile or a pattern, everything can be zoomed into and inspected in its foundational elements. Olenka's work explores the ways Graphic Design utilizes this principle to create highly expandable, adaptive, and responsive materials. From the individual qualities that unite a brand as a visual system, to the conceptual power a responsive design can add to a project, Olenka seeks to explore and become a master of applying this principle to everything she makes.
